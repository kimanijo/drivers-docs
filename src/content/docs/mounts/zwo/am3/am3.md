ZWO AM3 mounts are fully supported in INDI via both USB and WiFI connections. For connecting over WiFi, watch the  [WiFi Mounts tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uuDk1x7XgwE)

ZWO AM3 is identical to ZWO AM5 driver.
